Graduate Students


The VIP program is designed to engage sophomoroes, juniors, seniors, gradute students and faculty. Graduate students participate in one of two ways: enrolling for academic credit, or participating as mentors under the direction of their research advisors.

Enrolling for Academic Credit


The graduate students enroll in an aindependent study course with the instructor. If the instructor is from a different academic unit, the student will need to check with his/her academic advisor to confir mthe credits could be applied toward the student's degree. If the registration for the course is by permit only (which is often the case), the student should request a registration permit form the graduate program coordinator for the instructor's home department.
